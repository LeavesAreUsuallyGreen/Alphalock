
# Alphalock vs Mastermind: Deduction Through Constrained Feedback

At first glance:

**Alphalock** resembles **Wordle**

But conceptually:

Alphalock may have more in common with **Mastermind**.

---

# What Is Mastermind?

Mastermind is a classic deduction game where players guess hidden combinations and receive aggregate feedback about correctness.

Feedback is limited.

Players infer possibilities indirectly.

---

# Similarities Between Alphalock and Mastermind

Both emphasize:

✓ deduction

✓ elimination

✓ uncertainty reduction

✓ constrained feedback

✓ reasoning under ambiguity

In both games:

Knowing "something is correct"

does not always mean knowing:

what

or

where.

---

# Key Difference

Mastermind:

Typically uses colored pegs or symbols

Alphalock:

Uses words and language

This introduces additional complexity:

- word frequency
- vocabulary
- linguistic expectations

---

# Strategy Differences

Mastermind often rewards:

Systematic elimination

Alphalock may additionally reward:

- common word intuition
- linguistic reasoning
- adaptive guessing
