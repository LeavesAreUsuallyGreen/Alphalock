# Why Alphalock Can Feel Harder Than Wordle

Many players describe **Alphalock** as a more difficult **Wordle alternative**.

The primary reason:

**Less information per guess**

---

# Wordle Gives Positional Feedback

When guessing in Wordle, players learn:

✓ which letters appear

✓ where letters belong

✓ which letters are misplaced

This provides strong clues early.

---

# Alphalock Removes Positions

In Alphalock, players receive only aggregate counts:

- correct letters
- misplaced letters
- incorrect letters

without knowing positions.

You may know:

"2 letters are correct"

without knowing:

which 2

or where.

---

# Similarities to Mastermind

This makes Alphalock conceptually closer to:

**Mastermind**

than traditional Wordle.

Mastermind also emphasizes:

- constrained feedback
- deduction
- elimination
- uncertainty reduction

However:

Alphalock applies these ideas to words rather than colored pegs.

---

# More Ambiguity Means Different Thinking

Games emphasize different reasoning styles:

Wordle:

Pattern recognition + deduction

Mastermind:

Deduction + elimination

Alphalock:

Deduction + ambiguity + constrained feedback

Absurdle:

Adaptation against changing possibilities

---

# Harder Does Not Mean Better

Different games reward different skills.

Players seeking:

- difficult Wordle alternatives
- deductive puzzle games
- logic games
- Mastermind-like reasoning

may enjoy Alphalock.
