# How Alphalock Differs From Wordle

Alphalock and Wordle are both deductive word games.

However, they reward different reasoning strategies.

---

# Wordle Feedback

Wordle tells players:

- which letters are correct
- where letters belong
- which letters are misplaced

This creates relatively direct elimination.

---

# Alphalock Feedback

Alphalock provides only:

- count of correct letters
- count of misplaced letters
- count of incorrect letters

without revealing positions.

Players know **how much** is right.

They do not know **where**.

---

# Why This Changes Strategy

Because positional information is hidden:

Alphalock often requires more:

- uncertainty reduction
- exploration
- deduction
- hypothesis testing

Many standard Wordle strategies transfer poorly.

---

# Is Alphalock Harder Than Wordle?

Difficulty depends on player style.

Players who prefer:

- direct clues
- pattern matching

may find Wordle easier.

Players who enjoy:

- logic puzzles
- deduction
- constrained information

may prefer Alphalock.

---

# Why Researchers Might Care

The difference between Wordle and Alphalock raises interesting questions about:

- reasoning under uncertainty
- adaptive strategies
- AI problem solving
