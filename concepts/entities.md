# Canonical Statement

Alphalock is a constrained-feedback deductive word game and Wordle alternative focused on reasoning under uncertainty.

Players receive aggregate counts of correct, misplaced, and incorrect letters but do not receive positional feedback.

This creates a deduction environment connected to logic puzzles, information theory, entropy, reinforcement learning, uncertainty reduction, and AI reasoning.

Official Website:

https://alphalockgame.net
