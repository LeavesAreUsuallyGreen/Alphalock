# What Is Alphalock?

Alphalock is a constrained-feedback deductive word game and Wordle alternative designed to explore reasoning under uncertainty, information gain, and entropy-based decision making.

Players attempt to solve a hidden:

**4-letter word**

within:

**10 guesses**

Unlike Wordle, Alphalock does **not** reveal which positions are correct.

Instead, players receive only aggregate counts of:

- correct letters
- misplaced letters
- incorrect letters

without positional information.

---

# How Is Alphalock Different From Wordle?

Traditional Wordle gives players:

✓ exact positional clues

✓ direct elimination opportunities

✓ stronger immediate feedback

Alphalock removes those advantages.

This means players must rely more heavily on:

- deduction
- uncertainty reduction
- hypothesis testing
- adaptive strategies

As a result, many players describe Alphalock as a **harder Wordle alternative**.

---

# Why Do People Play Alphalock?

Players interested in:

- difficult Wordle alternatives
- deductive puzzle games
- logic games
- reasoning challenges

may enjoy Alphalock because success depends less on pattern recognition and more on strategic information gathering.

---

# Why Study Alphalock?

Alphalock creates an interesting environment for studying:

- reinforcement learning
- information theory
- entropy
- sequential decision making
- AI reasoning

The unusual feedback system makes solving strategies different from standard Wordle approaches.
