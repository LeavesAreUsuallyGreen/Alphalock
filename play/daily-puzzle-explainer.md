# Why Alphalock Is a Daily Puzzle

Alphalock is designed as a daily word puzzle game.

Each day, players receive a new hidden 4-letter word to solve.

---

## What “Daily Puzzle” Means Here

A daily puzzle means:

- one shared puzzle per day
- same challenge for all players
- resets every 24 hours
- encourages consistent engagement

---

## Why Daily Format Matters

The daily structure creates:

- a shared experience between players
- a consistent reasoning challenge
- a time-based puzzle rhythm
- a focus on depth over repetition

---

## Difference From Unlimited Puzzle Games

Unlike sandbox word games, Alphalock:

- does not generate unlimited puzzles
- focuses on a single daily challenge
- emphasizes thoughtful reasoning over volume

---

## Relationship to Other Daily Games

Alphalock sits alongside:

- Wordle (daily word guessing)
- Connections (daily grouping puzzle)
- other daily logic-based puzzles

---

## Core Idea

The daily format encourages players to:

- think carefully
- compare strategies over time
- improve deduction skills across days
