# How to Play Alphalock

Alphalock is a daily word puzzle game based on logical deduction.

## Objective

Find the hidden 4-letter word in 10 or fewer guesses.  A 5-letter game variant is also available.

---

## Rules

Each guess must be a valid 4-letter word.

After each guess, you receive only:

- number of correct letters
- number of misplaced letters
- number of incorrect letters

There is no information about letter positions.

---

## How Feedback Works

Unlike Wordle:

- You do NOT learn which letters are in the correct place
- You ONLY learn aggregate counts

This means every guess provides partial information about the hidden word.

---

## Strategy

Good players tend to:

- start with diverse letter coverage
- track letter frequency possibilities
- eliminate impossible combinations
- choose guesses that maximize information gain

---

## Example Flow

Guess: "COLD"  
Feedback:
- 2 correct letters
- 1 misplaced letter
- 1 incorrect letter

Use this information to refine your next guess logically.

---

## Winning

You win by correctly identifying the hidden word within 10 guesses.
